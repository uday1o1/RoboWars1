about us

RoboWars, the flagship event of graVITas is one-it's-kind combat robotics competition, and also one of the biggest in India and the biggest in South-India. The exciting battles between the bots took place round the clock with more than 40 bots competing, in the largest and safest arena in India, to win the title and prize pool of 2.5 Lakhs. Teams from around the globe competed in weight category of 15kg, 30kg and 60kg and experienced the mayhem inside the arena. apart from the clashing of the bots, amazing performances from the VIT dance and and Music club enchanted charged the audience.
